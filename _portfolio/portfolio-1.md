## Award: First Prize TKI Dinalog COVID-19 Challenge

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I worked on behalf of **GGD-GHOR Nederland**, **CWI**, and **Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam** in a team of Business Analytics students to optimize the distribution of vaccines across all vaccination centers during the first and second rounds of COVID-19 vaccinations.

### Recognition
Our work was awarded the **First Prize** in the TKI Dinalog COVID-19 Challenge, recognizing our contribution to solving critical logistics challenges during the pandemic.

**More information**: [VU News Article](https://vu.nl/nl/verhalen/vu-studenten-winnen-eerste-prijs-voor-vaccinatieplan)
